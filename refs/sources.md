## Source 1

**Author:** Hyndman, R. J., & Athanasopoulos, G.

**Year:** 2021

**Title:** Forecasting: Principles and Practice (3rd edition)

**Venue/Journal:** OTexts (Online Textbook)

**Key idea (2 cümle):**
This comprehensive textbook covers modern time series forecasting methods including ARIMA, exponential smoothing, and machine learning approaches. It provides practical guidance on model selection, evaluation metrics, and handling seasonality in energy consumption data.

**Why I use it (1 cümle):**
I use it as the foundational reference for implementing SARIMA models and understanding time series decomposition techniques for hourly energy forecasting.
